# Voice AI Overview

Chronos Studio Voice AI enables you to build sophisticated voice-enabled agents capable of natural conversational interactions.

## Capabilities

- **Real-time Voice**: Low-latency two-way conversation
- **Emotion Detection**: Understand caller emotional state
- **Telephony Integration**: Connect to phone networks
- **Custom Voices**: Branded voice experiences
- **Multilingual**: Support for multiple languages
